## Selection Guide

- For an Austrian PMA-style handbook, use the PMA handbook artifact map plus current pma/IPMA competence sources.
- For a global, value-oriented management frame, use PMBOK 8 and tailor its domains to the project.
- For organisation- and lifecycle-neutral guidance, use ISO 21502 while checking its post-review status.
- For open European governance and editable artifacts, use PM² v3.1 and the December 2025 artifact pack.
- For competence assessment or development, use IPMA ICB4.
- For an agile Austrian interpretation, add ICB4 in an Agile World.
- For Scrum delivery, use the official Scrum Guide and retain necessary project-level governance outside the Scrum framework.
- For AI-enabled work or an AI deliverable, add the 2026 PMI AI standard and the organisation's binding legal, privacy, security, data, IP, audit, and procurement rules.

These sources are complementary. Do not claim that one organisation endorses another organisation's method unless an official source says so.

## Practical Synthesis

Build a tailored operating model from four layers:

1. **Purpose and governance:** expected value, sponsor, decision rights, tolerances, funding, and benefits.
2. **Delivery system:** predictive, adaptive, or hybrid planning; work, dependencies, feedback, quality, and acceptance.
3. **Controls:** status, RAID, decisions, changes, resources, cost, procurement, assurance, transition, and closure.
4. **Competence and conduct:** leadership, collaboration, context, ethics, sustainability, security, and responsible AI use.

Keep the layers explicit. A competence model does not define the schedule; a delivery framework does not authorise funding; a template does not prove that control is operating.

## Freshness Rules

1. Browse the official source before making a "latest", certification, compliance, or conformance claim.
2. Record the publication version, source page, source date, and access date.
3. Distinguish publication date, web-page modification date, file metadata, errata date, and review stage.
4. Prefer a stable official landing page over an unverified mirror.
5. Mark announced or draft material as such.
6. When a standard is paid or copyrighted, cite and paraphrase within lawful limits rather than reproducing it.
7. If two official pages conflict, state the conflict and use the narrower claim until the owner clarifies it.
