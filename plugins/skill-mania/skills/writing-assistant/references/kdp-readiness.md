# KDP Readiness Reference

Use this for Kindle/KDP readiness, front matter, Look Inside, metadata, categories, pricing, and launch-readiness review.

## Verification Rule

KDP platform rules, category options, pricing bands, royalty details, file requirements, and metadata policies can change. When exact current requirements matter, verify against current official Amazon KDP documentation before giving final instructions.

## Editorial Readiness

Check:

- opening pages deliver the book's promise quickly
- table of contents and chapter titles look intentional
- front matter does not delay the reader unnecessarily
- ending supports review/referral behavior
- prose and formatting are consistent enough for paid publication

## Package Readiness

Check:

- title and subtitle match the actual book
- cover promise matches the reading experience
- series name and numbering are clear
- print and ebook positioning are aligned
- acknowledgments, author note, citations, or disclaimers are present when needed

## Metadata And Store Page

Review:

- description/blurb clarity
- category fit
- keyword naturalness
- author bio
- age/grade suitability where relevant
- pricing relative to length, category, and format

Do not optimize for rank tricks at the expense of reader fit.
